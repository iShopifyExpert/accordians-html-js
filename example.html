  • Leather Care

    Leather is a natural material. Minor color changes and discrepancies on its surface are not quality defects. They are part of the nature of the genuine leather products. For keeping your leather in great shape;

    - Be careful not to scratch or rub your product against abrasives surfaces.
    - Protect your product from humidity and keep it away from direct sources of heat, such as radiators and the inside of a car in summer.
    - If the leather gets dirty, or in instance of superficial stains on the lining, we recommend that you gently wipe it with a soft and light-colored cloth. Never use soap or solvent.
    - For a deeper clean, we suggest using something like our Bomonti Cleaner & Conditioner and Cream that will help preserve the natural lubricating oils instead of stripping them.
